The Strategic Context

The global narcotics trade has evolved into one of the most powerful drivers of transnational crime, increasingly intersecting with terrorism, organized criminal networks, and illicit financial systems.

From the Golden Crescent to Latin America, the nexus between narcotics trafficking and terrorism has emerged as a critical challenge for global security, undermining governance and fueling violent conflicts.
